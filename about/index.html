Brief Bio

Jinyu Yu was awarded the Bachelor’s degree of Computer Science and Technology from Huazhong University of Science and Technologyin June, 2014. He is currently a PhD Candidate for Computer Science and Technology in Data Storage and Application Laboratory, WNLO. And his supervisor is Professor Dan Feng and Assistant Professor Wei Tong. His current research interests include Fibre Channel over Ethernet(FCoE), I/O Virtualization, Virtual Machine Migration, Software Defined Storage(SDS), Docker and Kubernetes. He is a student member of ACM and CCF.

Education experience

  • 2014 - Now: Doctor of Computer Science and Technology, in WNLO
  • 2010 - 2014: Bachelor of Computer Science and Technology, in School of Computer and Technology, HUST
